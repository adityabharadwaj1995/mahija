How Our Chit Fund Works

Understanding the simple and transparent process of participating in our chit fund

1

Choose Your Plan

Select a chit plan that aligns with your financial goals, ranging from ₹5 Lakhs to ₹40 Lakhs.

2

Monthly Contributions

Make regular monthly installments as per your chosen plan for the duration of 40 months.

3

Auction Process

Participate in monthly auctions where subscribers bid for the chit amount. The lowest bidder wins.

4

Receive Dividends

Non-winning subscribers receive dividends from the auction discount, growing your savings.

5

Financial Flexibility

Access funds when needed or continue saving to maximize returns over the plan duration.

What is a Chit Fund?

A chit fund is a financial instrument that combines savings and credit. It is a unique Indian financial product where a group of individuals come together to contribute a fixed amount regularly. Each month, the pooled amount is given to one member through an auction process.

Key Benefits of Participation

Disciplined Savings

Regular monthly contributions help build a savings habit.

Access to Lump Sum

Win auctions to get funds for urgent financial needs.

Dividend Earnings

Receive dividends from auction discounts every month.

Flexible Duration

Choose plans with manageable 40-month duration.
