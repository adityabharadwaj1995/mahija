Our Identity

Who We Are

Mahija Chit Fund Pvt Ltd is a premier financial institution in Telangana, established with a vision to provide secure, transparent, and community-driven savings solutions. We operate under the stringent guidelines of the Chit Fund Act and the Indian Companies Act, 1956.

We act as a bridge between your current savings and your future financial goals, offering a platform where trust meets growth. Our journey is defined by thousands of families who have achieved their dreams through our disciplined chit plans.

Our Core Philosophy

"To empower every household with a habit of regular savings and provide immediate liquidity during financial needs, ensuring no dream is left unfulfilled due to lack of funds."
